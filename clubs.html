Current Club Information

The following organizations are recognized by the Ardrey Kell ICC and are student-led and staff supported. Each has been developed by students in collaboration with staff advisors. Contact the staff advisor for additional details.

We have placed the clubs into six different categories. This will assist you as seek out your desired club involvement. When choosing which club(s) to participate in, remember it is best to have a diverse portfolio of involvement. Also, please note that some clubs can fall under more than one category; however, we have chosen the category that best define's each club's mission. Check out each club's description to get a full idea of what involvement in each club entails.
